Manufacturing & Logistics

Hapur’s industrial story is propelled by good quality wide roads, rail freight corridors and policy reforms. It stands adjacently to the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor (EDFC) route map and benefits from the Western DFC creating multimodal interfaces for containerized and bulk cargo.
On roads, the NH 9 Hapur–Moradabad six laning and DME’s Dasna–Hapur spur deliver predictable transit times. Upcoming Ganga Expressway further opens land availability and logistics competitiveness—UPEIDA’s progress reports show high completion levels and bidder awards for concession groups, indicating near term operation.

Sector Anchors

Historically, Hapur is known for stainless steel pipes & tubes and Pilkhuwa’s handloom/powerloom home furnishings, both supported under UP’s ODOP program. New corridors enable light engineering, textiles finishing, packaging, and agri processing clusters. UPSIDA’s statewide capex surge (₹551 Cr in 2024 25; +430% vs 2018 19) evidences accelerated industrial area upgrades, which spill over into HPDA’s jurisdiction via ecosystem improvements and supplier base development.

Local Pipeline & Sites

HPDA lists proposed industrial areas: Dheer Khera, NH 24 Bypass Hapur, Textile Centre Pilkhuwa, Reliance Road Pilkhuwa, Lakhan, Tatarpur—with Master Plan demarcations. Land acquisition progress, ekupat approvals, and scheme wise plots, indicates near term site readiness.

Good Regulatory & Incentives environment at State level

UP’s policy stack—Industrial Investment & Employment Promotion Policy (2022), Textile & Garmenting Policy (2022), Warehousing & Logistics Policy (2022), EV Manufacturing (2022), MSME Promotion (2022)—is delivered via Nivesh Mitra and Nivesh Sarathi, backed by BRAP reforms. Media and DPIIT sources consistently place UP among Achiever States on EoDB implementation—with thousands of compliances simplified or decriminalized—reducing transaction costs for manufacturers.

Opportunities to Consider

• Steel downstream (fabrication, fittings) harnessing Hapur’s pipes/tubes legacy.
• Home furnishing manufacturing & export finishing in Pilkhuwa, integrating ODOP branding and design institutes.
• 3PL warehousing & cold chain along NH 9/DME nodes with DFC linkages; benefit from UP’s logistics park policy thrust.
