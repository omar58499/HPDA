Hotels & Tourism

Riverfront, Pilgrimage & MICE

(MICE = Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions)

Hapur’s Garh Mukteshwar is steeped in spiritual heritage and sits on the Ganga. HPDA understands the potential for riverfront development, international grade amusement parks, and global recognition for ancient temples—creating a pipeline for hospitality assets from budget stays to boutique riverfront resorts.

Access & Flow

The DME delivers fast NCR tourist flow; NH 9 and the Ganga Expressway will stitch western and eastern UP circuits, converting day trip potential into overnight stays. That unlocks demand for highway hotels, pilgrimage lodges, eco retreats, and festival season capacity.

MICE & Events

HPDA’s ongoing civic upgrades and upcoming Convention Centre within Anand Vihar signal prospects for MICE, weddings, and social events. With 1 hour access from Delhi for many segments, Hapur can host value priced MICE displacing higher cost NCR venues, especially for public sector, associations, and MSME conclaves.

Experience Themes

• Riverfront & Pilgrimage: Boutique hotels/lodges near Garh with prayer deck designs; curated temple circuits; Ganga aarti experiences; seasonal mela linked capacity.
• Textile Heritage: Pilkhuwa tours—handloom printing workshops; home furnishing markets; export houses; craft studios—paired with experiential stays.
• Highway Business Hotels: DME/NH 9 nodes targeted at corporate crews, logistics, and project teams.

Tangible opportunities

• 3 star business hotels at DME exits / NH 9 junctions.
• Riverfront resorts & wellness retreats in Garh Mukteshwar with Ayurvedic spa, yoga/meditation.
• MICE center hotels near HPDA schemes to capture government, education, and MSME events.

Combined, these create a diversified hospitality portfolio hedging seasonality and tapping spiritual, leisure, and corporate flows.
